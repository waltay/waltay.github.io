Middle-Fork White-Water Rafting

This incredible adventures takes place in Middle-Fork at Salmon River. It lasts for 3 - 4 days camping near the river banks. Our guides will help you navigate through these white-waters and have a thrilling experience while still feeling safe. For this experience you have to book for at least 6 people to fill up one raft. This experience costs $300.00 per person which includes all the equipment necessary to safely navigate through these waters and a guide.
