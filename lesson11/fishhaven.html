Bear Lake

By Bear Fish

When you come to Fish Haven, you can't miss out on Bear Lake! Literally, we're right on the shore of it. There's plenty of activities you can participate at or around Bear Lake any time of the year. You can dive into the intense turquoise blue water that is Bear Lake, jet ski, go sailing, boating, fish, swim and play on our sandy beaches. Did you know that Bear Lake is known as the “Caribbean of the Rockies”. During winter, you can go snowmobiling, ice fishing and snow skiing. Plus, any time of the year you can go hiking on the amazing trails surrounding this landscape and enjoy of the amazing fauna that inhabits by bird watching and running into the wildlife. We even have locations where you can go golfing in this beautiful location. Don't miss out on this incredible experience and visit Bear Lake.
