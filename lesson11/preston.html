When in Preston...

By Activarios Prestonus

Welcome to Preston! Whether you are visiting or a local resident, there are plenty of places to visit and activities to do. Here are some of the best ones you should definitely not miss out. First, if you're an adventurer who likes to connect with nature and enjoy being outdoors, we have several trails you can hike. Two of the best that you should definitely give a try are the "Willow Flat Springs Trail" and the "Maple Creek Spring" trail. If you're looking for aquatic adventures, head on over to Riverdale Resort Hot Springs. It includes a large open swimming pool and hot tubs fed by natural hot water along with some water slides. Finally, if you really enjoyed Napoleon Dynamite, Preston is where it all happened. You could plan a scavenger hunt to visit all the iconic locations that appear in the movie. Make sure to check the weather in advance to planning any of your activities to make sure your fun doesn't get spoiled.
