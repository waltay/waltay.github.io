About Us

Our Purpose

At the Provo Chamber of Commerce we seek to strengthen the economic climate in the city in a way that will help grow not only current businesses, but also allow growth for the coming generations. We aim to secure the business' interests of all sectors and businesses of all sizes in the city. By doing so, we help the city and its businesses to prosper and in turn help increase the quality of life of everyone in the city.

Our Goals

To achieve our purpose we have goals such as working with local government to advocate for and foster policies that will enable businesses to open and thrive in the area. We create an environment which facilitates networking amongst members helping small businesses grow, and we promote innovation and entrepreneurship giving access to our members to resources and opportunities that will allow them to innovate and grow.

Companies of the Week

Lakeside Automotive

Lakeside Automotive is known for not only creating an amazing workplace environment, but also providing excellent service to all their customers at affordable prices. Their excellent service goes as far as picking up customers when their cars are ready so they don't have to walk.

The Man Barber

This barber shop's haircuts are trendy and current, but the techniques used are as old as the profession itself. With traditional techniques both for your hair and beard, coming to this shop is an experience itself. They have barbers which each specialize in different styles, so you can be sure that you'll get the cut you want.

Park Animal Hospital

This veterinary hospital is the perfect place to ensure the health of your furry friends. With qualified doctors, you know you're pets will be in the best of hands. Not only that, but this hospital doubles as a pet resort. So, whether in need of medical attention or somewhere to house your pet as you go on a trip, Park Animal Hospital is the perfect choice.
