Target Audience

The website is meant for those needing to know what the weather will be like that day, or in the following days, in the towns of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven in Idaho. It will also be for those needing to report storms in these towns.

Personas

Anne

Anne is a college student, age 20. She studies in BYU-Idaho in Rexburg, but is planning a weekend trip to Fish Haven with her friends. Like most people her age, she looks up almost everything on her phone. She wants to know what the weather will be like on the weekend to see if it will be worth going this specific weekend. On the website, she also finds it contains information about some of the main activities to do in the area.

Roger

Roger is 43 years old and lives in Preston. He has a wife and 2 kids. He works in the area and daily has to commute to work. Every morning he checks on his laptop the weather for the day to make sure he has taken the necessary precautions for the day for him and his family.

Scenarios

  • Do I need to take a rain coat and umbrella to school today?
  • What upcoming events are there in Soda Springs?
  • Will the weather be clear this weekend to go to Bear Lake?
  • Is it snowing today? If so, I need to leave early to work.
  • There's a storm that wasn't forecasted. I better report it.
