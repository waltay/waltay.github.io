The Weather Pitstop

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to provide current weather data for local areas in Idaho as well as forecasted weather data. This will help users prepare for the day and take necessary precautions when needed. It will also provide information and images into different weather phenomena, allowing users to learn more information about weather. Along with this, there will be information about the town for those who wish to learn more about it.
